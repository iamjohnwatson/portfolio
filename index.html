CHAPTER 6: MY APPLICATION

Cover Letter

Dear Ella,

I am writing to apply for the Financial Graphics Journalist position with Reuters Graphics. My expertise in the Technology, Media, and Telecom sectors at Reuters News, combined with my passion for data visualization, makes me an ideal candidate for this role.

During my 3.5-year tenure at Reuters, I have developed a strong foundation in financial reporting, particularly in the technology sector. I've led coverage of electric vehicle companies, including Tesla, Rivian, and Lucid, consistently breaking news and developing deep industry knowledge. What has set my reporting apart is my commitment to incorporating graphics into my stories. I have actively developed data visualization skills and mentored colleagues on how to integrate graphics into their reporting to make complex financial information more accessible to our audience.

Beyond my reporting experience, I bring valuable technical skills to the table. I have completed Google's Professional Data Analytics certification, where I gained proficiency in programming languages and visualization techniques. I am comfortable working with complex datasets and have experience creating custom graphics without relying on off-the-shelf tools. Additionally, my background in media production, including podcast hosting and video editing, has honed my visual storytelling abilities across multiple formats.

I have taught myself coding languages like HTML, CSS and Javascript over the past year and worked on data visualization, web development projects as a hobby. In fact, inspired by the Reuters Graphics' Cozy video games, I made a retro-style arcade game for the web called Treasure Collector in a marathon coding session over a weekend in early February.

What excites me most about joining the Reuters Graphics team is the opportunity to fully merge my financial reporting expertise with my passion for visual communication. I am drawn to your team's reputation for creativity, collaboration, and innovative storytelling. I am particularly interested in creating data-rich visual stories that illuminate the complex relationships between economics, business, politics, and other critical areas. My experience covering the technology sector gives me unique insights into how these fields intersect and impact global markets.

Thank you for considering my application. I would love the opportunity to discuss how my skills and experience could contribute to the Reuters financial graphics team.

Sincerely,

Akash Sriram
