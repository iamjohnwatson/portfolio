Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in the Financial Graphics Journalist position with Reuters Graphics. As a current Sector Specialist in Technology, Media, and Telecom at Reuters News with a passion for data visualization, I believe my unique combination of financial reporting expertise and technical skills makes me an ideal candidate for this role.

During my tenure at Reuters, I have developed a strong foundation in financial reporting, particularly in the technology sector. I've led coverage of electric vehicle companies including Tesla, Rivian, and Lucid, consistently breaking news and developing deep industry knowledge. What sets my reporting apart is my commitment to incorporating data visualization to enhance storytelling. I have actively developed these skills and assisted colleagues in implementing graphics in their stories, making complex financial information more accessible to our audience.

Beyond my reporting experience, I bring valuable technical skills to the table. I have completed Google's Professional Data Analytics certification, where I gained proficiency in programming languages and visualization techniques. I can work comfortably with datasets in Excel and Google Sheets, and I have experience creating custom graphics without relying on off-the-shelf tools. My background in media production, including podcast hosting and video editing, has honed my visual storytelling abilities across multiple formats.

What excites me most about joining the Reuters Graphics team is the opportunity to fully merge my financial reporting expertise with my passion for visual communication. I am drawn to your team's reputation for innovation, creativity, and collaboration, and I share your belief that treating colleagues with flexibility and empathy leads to the best work.

I am particularly interested in creating data-rich visual stories that illuminate the complex relationships between economics, business, and other critical areas like technology and the environment. I believe my experience covering the technology sector gives me unique insights into how these fields intersect and impact global markets.

Thank you for considering my application. I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my skills and experience could contribute to Reuters Graphics' continued excellence in visual financial journalism.

Sincerely,

Akash Sriram
